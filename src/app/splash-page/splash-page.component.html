Reactive State, Secured by Design.

This is the ngVault Demo — an interactive showcase of reactive state management for Angular.

Here, you can explore how signals, stores, and state synchronization come together to power dynamic applications.

Use the Examples section to view live user and car data, see instant updates, and understand how ngVault connects related entities in real time.

Then open the Dev Tools panel to inspect what’s happening under the hood — every event, patch, and state change — giving you a clear, hands-on view of how ngVault manages and reacts to application state.
